" ( brazilcha portugalcha talaffuzi: [nejˈmaʁ dɐ ˈsiwvɐ ˈsɐ̃tus ˈʒũɲoʁ] ⓘ; born 5 February 1992), also known as Neymar Júnior or simply Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a left winger, attacking midfielder or forward for Campeonato Brasileiro Série A team Santos, whom he captains, and the Brazil national team. Often praised as one of the best players of all time, he is known for his showboating skills, dribbling, playmaking, and finishing.[8] He is Brazil's all-time top goalscorer. Neymar made his professional debut with Santos in 2009. In 2011, he was awarded the league's Best Player and won the FIFA Puskás Award for a solo effort in the league. In the same year, he helped Santos win their first Copa Libertadores in nearly 50 years.[9] In 2013, he joined Barcelona and became part of an attacking trio with Lionel Messi and Luis Suárez, dubbed MSN. Winning the continental treble of La Liga, the Copa del Rey, and the UEFA Champions League in the trio's first season, Neymar was the joint-top goalscorer of the Champions League campaign and top goalscorer of the Copa del Rey. In his final season, Neymar reached 100 goals for Barcelona and set the record for most assists in a Champions League campaign. During his time at Barcelona, he was named in the FIFA FIFPro World11 twice and made the podium for the FIFA Ballon d'Or in 2015 and 2017. Asosiy o'yinchi bo'lishga undagan Neymar 2017 yilda PSJga 222 million evro evaziga qo'shildi va u tarixdagi eng qimmat futbolchiga aylandi . [ 1-eslatma ] To'liq pleymeykerga aylanib, u PSJning Liga 1 chempionligini davom ettirishiga katta hissa qo'shdi va beshta chempionlikni qo'lga kiritdi. Individual tarzda u debyut mavsumida yilning eng yaxshi futbolchisi deb topilgan va uch marta Yil jamoasiga kiritilgan. Chempionlar ligasida u PSJning 2020-yilda birinchi finaliga chiqishida ajralmas bo‘ldi, o‘sha yili UEFA tomonidan yilning eng yaxshi jamoasi nomiga kirdi va o‘zini Chempionlar ligasida eng ko‘p gol urgan braziliyalik sifatida ko‘rsatdi. PSJ uchun 100 dan ortiq gol urgan u uchta turli klubda kamida 100 ta gol urgan to'rtinchi futbolchi bo'ldi. Jarohatlarning davom etishi PSJning 2023 yilda Neymarni “Al Hilol” ga 90 million yevroga sotishiga sabab bo‘ldi , bu Saudiya Pro-ligasi rekordi bo‘ldi. U bir yillik ACL jarohati tufayli kamdan-kam o'ynadi va 2025 yil yanvar oyida o'zining bolaligidagi "Santos" klubiga qaytdi. 18 yoshida Neymar Braziliya terma jamoasi safida debyut qildi. U 128 ta o'yinda 79 ta gol urgan mamlakat tarixidagi eng yaxshi to'purar hisoblanadi va ko'pchilik o'z avlodidagi eng yaxshi braziliyalik futbolchi sifatida e'tirof etiladi. U 2013 yilgi FIFA Konfederatsiyalar kubogini qo'lga kiritib , "Oltin to'p"ni qo'lga kiritdi . 2014 yilgi futbol bo'yicha jahon chempionatida u Dream Team tarkibiga kirdi . U 2016 yilgi yozgi Olimpiada o'yinlarida erkaklar futboli bo'yicha Braziliya terma jamoasi sardori bo'lib, 2012 yilda kumush medalni qo'lga kiritgan edi . U 2018 yilgi Jahon chempionatida maydonga tushdi va uch yildan so'ng Braziliyaga 2021 yilgi Amerika Kubogida Argentinaga ikkinchi o'rinni egallashga yordam berdi va birgalikda "Eng yaxshi o'yinchi" nominatsiyasida taqdirlandi . 2022 yilgi Jahon chempionatiga tayyorgarlik ko'rish jarayonida Neymar 12 ta gol urib, FIFA Jahon chempionati saralash o'yinlarida Braziliyaning barcha vaqtlardagi eng yaxshi to'purariga aylandi . 2022 yilgi jahon chempionatida u Pele va Ronaldudan keyin uchta jahon chempionatida gol urgan uchinchi braziliyalik futbolchiga aylandi . U rekord darajadagi oltita Samba Gold mukofotini qo'lga kiritdi."
